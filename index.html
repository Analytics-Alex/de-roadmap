⚠ Reality Check

These timelines assume 2–3 focused hours per day and building real projects alongside learning — not just watching tutorials. Most people take longer. Phases overlap; you do not finish one before starting another. Your SQL warehouse work maps to late Phase 1 / early Phase 2 — you are not starting from zero.

There is no finish line. Senior data engineers are still learning. The goal is employability at each phase, not completion of everything.
